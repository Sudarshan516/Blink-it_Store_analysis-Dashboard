# Blink-it_Store_analysis-Dashboard
To conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to identify key insights and opportunities for optimization using various KPIs and visualizations in Power BI.
# Data_Set Used:
-<a href= "https://github.com/Sudarshan516/Blink-it_Store_analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/BlinkIT%20Grocery%20Data.xlsx">Blink it Grocery Data</a>
# KPI's Requirements:

1. Total Sales: The overall revenue generated from all items sold.
2. Average Sales: The average revenue per sale.
3. Number of Items: The total count of different items sold.
4. Average Rating: The average customer rating for items sold.

# Project Insights

* The total sales generated are $1.20M from 8,523 items, with an average rating of 3.92.
* Sales increased steadily over the years and reached the highest point in 2018.
* Medium-sized outlets contribute the highest revenue compared to small and large outlets.
* Tier 3 locations generate more sales than Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities.
* Fruits, snacks, and household items are the top-selling product categories.
* Supermarket Type 1 outlets contribute the largest share of total sales

# Conclusion
The Blinkit Sales Dashboard reveals that revenue growth is strongly influenced by outlet size, location tier, and product category. Medium-sized outlets in Tier 3 cities are the most profitable segments, while daily consumable categories drive consistent revenue.

The business maintains stable customer satisfaction and strong sales performance, with clear opportunities for expansion, inventory optimization, and targeted growth strategies.

This dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by identifying high-performing segments, growth opportunities, and operational improvement areas.
